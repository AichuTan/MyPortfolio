About Me

I’m Aichu Tan, a data enthusiast with a background in engineering and a passion for geospatial technology. Before pursuing my graduate studies in Big Data Analytics at SDSU, I worked as a process engineer in the DRAM industry and later founded an online clothing business. These experiences shaped my analytical thinking and entrepreneurial spirit.

At SDSU, I’ve been exploring the intersection of data science, web technologies, and machine learning. I’m particularly drawn to projects that have real-world impact — such as improving farming outcomes using AI or making public data more accessible through visualization.

In my free time, I enjoy hiking, experimenting with new coding tools, and contributing to open-source projects that promote sustainability.
